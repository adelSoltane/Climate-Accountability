As citizens who are concerned about our climate, researching the issue comes with its challenges. Often what comes up from a quick Google search is either very vague statements about "needing to reduce carbon emissions" or very specialized scientific articles containing information that we lack the expertise to actually understand. If you have also faced this issue, we invite you to browse our website. Here, you’ll be able to visualize climate data and check on politician’s promises, no expertise needed.
